Welcome to Projects

Since you’re here, check out some of the work I’ve done so far.

Out & About

Out&Abt is a web app that helps immigrants in NYC find housing and work while overcoming language barriers. It offers curated resources, multilingual support, and personalized recommendations.

Ghibli Universe

Ghibli Universe is a simple and responsive web application that fetches and displays films from the Studio Ghibli API. Users can search for films, click on movie cards to view more details, and interact with a "See More" button to reveal hidden descriptions.

CTPH

Collaborated with Conservation Through Public Health (CTPH), a Ugandan nonprofit using a One Health model to link wildlife and human health. Redesigned their cluttered WordPress site to better communicate their key programs.
