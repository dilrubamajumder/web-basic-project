About Me

I am a passionate and versatile designer with some knowledge of full-stack development, particularly with the PERN stack. Currently pursuing a degree in Human-Computer Interaction, Interaction Design, and Product Design, I am deeply interested in creating intuitive and impactful user experiences. My technical expertise spans React.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and a variety of frontend and backend technologies, complemented by a strong focus on design principles and user-centered solutions. With experience in building projects such as a to-do list app and exploring APIs, I am always eager to learn, solve complex problems, and build products that simplify and delight users. I aim to continue growing my skills, particularly in UX design, project management, and data structures, while keeping a keen focus on delivering quality, functional, and visually appealing digital solutions.
